            What Is Iwata Asks

            Iwata Asks is a series of interviews conducted by former Nintendo Global President Satoru Iwata with key creators behind the making of Nintendo games and hardware. Legendary figures such as Mario™ creator Shigeru Miyamoto are often involved in these in-depth discussions.
