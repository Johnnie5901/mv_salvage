Play Splatoon 2 at GameStop on 7/22

The squid kids called Inklings are back to splat more ink and claim more turf in this colorful and chaotic action shooter.

For the first time, take Turf War battles on-the-go with the Nintendo Switch™ system.

Experience the fun and play Splatoon 2 for Nintendo Switch and receive a free Splatoon 2 color changing cup* this Saturday, July 22nd at select GameStop stores from 12pm-3pm (local time).

For more information on Splatoon 2, visit the official site at http://splatoon.nintendo.com/

*Must play Splatoon 2 demo to receive free cup. 1 per person while supplies last. Cup colors vary. No purchase necessary.

Demos available at participating locations. See retailer for more details. Opportunity to play will be on a first come, first served basis, while time permits.

Game Rated:
