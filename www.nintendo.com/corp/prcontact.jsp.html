Public Relations Contact

Media professionals seeking a press or public relations contact at Nintendo of America, please submit your "Request for Information" below. Please do not submit a request which requires a response in less than 24 hours. If you need immediate assistance, please contact Nintendo's PR department at 425.882.2040.

This is for US based media only. If you are non-US media, please contact the appropriate subsidiary at http://www.nintendo.com/global. Due to the high-volume of requests, we cannot guarantee a response to non-U.S. requests.

If you are not a member of the news media, please e-mail us by clicking here.
