The following are Health & Safety Precautions manuals for Nintendo's home and portable systems. It is recommended that you read these manuals before using any of our products.

If you are having problems viewing these documents, or you would rather have hard copies sent to you, please e-mail us by clicking here. Be sure to include your full name and mailing address with your request.

We offer the files in two different formats: HTML (standard webpages that can be opened and printed like most pages on the internet), and .PDFs (Adobe Acrobat files, which require Acrobat Reader by Adobe.*)
