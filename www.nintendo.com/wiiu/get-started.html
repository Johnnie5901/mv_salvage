See what your Wii U
can do

You already know your Wii U console plays great games, but here are a few more ways to make the most of your new system.

Earn points on digital purchases with the My Nintendo rewards program and redeem them for fun rewards!

Make Mii™ character versions of your family and friends to use in Miiverse™ and specially-designed Wii U games.

Add someone to your Friend List so you can play together in compatible games.

Download a free demo or find downloadable content (DLC) for select games on Nintendo eShop.

Add fun in-game extras (like bonus levels and more) with amiibo™ accessories1.

Add a game to your Nintendo eShop Wish List.

Find a great screenshot or drawing on Miiverse™ and give it a "Yeah!"

Watch a video on YouTube, Netflix, Amazon Instant Video, or Hulu Plus2.

Browse the Internet on your system.

Find both new and classic games on Nintendo eShop, so you always have something to play!


1. Compatibility and functionality of amiibo may vary per game. Software updates may be required. Visit amiibo.com for specific details on how each amiibo works.
2. Paid membership may be required. Visit YouTube, Netflix, Amazon Instant Video, or Hulu Plus game detail pages for details.
