FEATURES

The Nintendo 3DS Image Share service makes it easy to show off screenshots from compatible games and applications, including Animal Crossing™: Happy Home Designer, Animal Crossing™: New Leaf – Welcome amiibo, Tomodachi Life, and Photos with Mario. Share to Twitter or Facebook—or both at the same time.

Please note: posting functions to Tumblr from Nintendo 3DS, 3DS XL, New Nintendo 3DS, New Nintendo 3DS XL, and 2DS are unavailable.

Compatible with games such as:

* Your Nintendo 3DS system needs to be connected to the Internet.
* You must have an account in order to use Twitter or Facebook. Please note that you may only post images and comments using Nintendo 3DS Image Share
You must access your Twitter or Facebook accounts separately to update account information or edit and/or delete your posts.

HOW TO ACCESS
Check out https://i.nintendo.net from the Internet Browser on your Nintendo 3DS system
From QR Code
You can also access the Image Share site by taking a photo of this QR code with your Nintendo 3DS.

Scan the QR Code on the left with the camera of Nintendo 3DS to access the site.
1) Press L or R Button on your Nintendo 3DS to activate the camera on HOME Menu.
2) Touch the on the bottom left
3) Scan the QR Code on the left with the camera
From Nintendo 3DS Bookmarks
Open “Nintendo 3DS BookMarks” from Favorites in the Internet Browser and click on the link within the page.
HOW TO POST PHOTOS

  • Step 1


    Open the Internet Browser from your Nintendo 3DS HOME Menu, tap the URL button and go to http://i.nintendo.net/.

  • Step 2


    Log in to your Twitter or Facebook account.

  • Step 3


    Tap the “Browse…” button to access the in-game photos you’ve taken with your Nintendo 3DS.

  • Step 4


    Select your image, add a comment, and press “Post.” That’s it!
