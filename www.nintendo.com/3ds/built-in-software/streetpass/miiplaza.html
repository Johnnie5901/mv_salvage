StreetPass Mii Plaza

The StreetPass Mii Plaza is where Mii™ characters meet and greet each other. Just be sure to carry your Nintendo 3DS system with you and see how many Mii characters you can collect. Then stop by StreetPass Mii Plaza to meet them and play fun games.

New!
  • Use the new quick plaza to select each StreetPass Mii Plaza game quickly.
  • Store up to 100 Mii characters at the Plaza Gate with purchase of Mii Plaza premium.
  • In Puzzle Swap, you'll have a chance to score multiple pieces at a time (Mii Plaza Premium required).

Play with new characters in games powered by the Mii characters you collect

NEW Premium content!

Buy together and save

Go to StreetPass Mii Plaza and choose either Slot Car Rivals or Market Crashers for free. Purchase any of the remaining new StreetPass games individually for $2.99 each or as a bundle for $8.99.

Slot Car Rivals

Race against StreetPass rivals to become the World Champion of the slot car circuit! After the race, your rivals will create a new boost for your car—the more rivals, the better booster you'll get!

Market Crashers

Buy and sell virtual stocks with the advice of Mii characters you’ve met via StreetPass, and see how much in-game currency you can earn!

Ninja Launcher

Launch yourself from a cannon and collect airborne items to defeat demons at the end of your path. The more items you collect, the more powerful you become.

Feed Mii

Cook for heroes you meet via StreetPass so that they can defeat foes on a full stomach! Check how well the heroes faired in their battles and read food reviews from the Legendary Herald news.

Mii Trek

Gather a band of explorers for a trek through ancient ruins full of sacred treasures, hidden paths, deadly foes, and more.

Premium content

Battleground Z

Gather weapons, help fellow survivors, and show those zombies who’s boss!

Flower Town

Grow and maintain your own flowers and cross-pollinate with flowers from other StreetPass Mii characters.

Ultimate Angler

Head to the StreetPass Islands, and go fishing with the Mii characters you’ve met via StreetPass.

Warrior's Way

Collect troops over StreetPass and fight other warlords to conquer new countries.

Mii Force

Blast your way through enemies in this scrolling space shooter.

Monster Manor

Design and explore a mansion using pieces that you collect from StreetPass Mii characters.

Free content

Find Mii

In this RPG-style quest, your strategy and the Mii characters you encounter through Streetpass are your only hope to save your Mii character from the Dark Lord!

Puzzle Swap

Collect puzzle pieces for panels featuring Mario™, The Legend of Zelda™, Pokémon™ and more. When you finish a panel, you can view it (in 3D on a Nintendo 3DS or Nintendo 3DS XL system or in 2D on Nintendo 2DS) and check out an animated surprise!

How to purchase

  • 1

    Make sure you have the most recent version of StreetPass Mii Plaza. Follow the prompts on your Nintendo 3DS system to accept the update.

  • 2

    Once your system restarts after the update, go to StreetPass Mii Plaza and select "Games."

  • 3

    Select the bunny character next to the game title you're interested in to learn more or purchase the game.

  • 4

    After you purchase a game with funds from your Nintendo eShop account, it will automatically download and install, and then appear in your "Games" menu.

Other fun stuff to make your Mii & Mii Plaza uniquely yours

Customize your Mii Character

Decorate your speech balloon with images from some of your favorite franchises, and earn outfits and Nintendo-inspired headgear! Complete challenges in games to receive tickets, then redeem them in StreetPass Mii Plaza to unlock a new look.

Your plaza, your way.

You can upgrade your Mii Plaza for $4.99 on the Nintendo eShop. Already upgraded? No problem—you'll receive this update for free!

What's included:
  • Store up to 100 Mii™ characters at the Plaza Gate, allowing for more encounters at events.
  • New in-game pin to honor your purchase of the Mii Plaza Premium.

StreetPass Relay Points

Head to a Nintendo Zone™ location to collect up to six Mii™ characters.
