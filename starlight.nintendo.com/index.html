To date, more than 7,000 Fun Centers have been sponsored by companies, foundations and individuals, and are being enjoyed by thousands of pediatric patients at hospitals across North America. We understand the importance of keeping your Fun Centers up and running to provide continued support and endless hours of entertainment and distraction for the patients in your hospitals. With that in mind, Starlight teamed up with Nintendo of America to provide you a place where you can access additional interactive information on how to troubleshoot and further benefit from the Fun Centers in your hospital.

Thank you for your continued support of the Fun Center program!

Sincerely,
Starlight Children's Foundation and Nintendo of America Inc.
